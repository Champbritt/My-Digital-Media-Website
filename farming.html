This is the Farming Page

Wheat

Food Points: 5, only when made into bread

Seed: Seeds [from breaking long grass].

Other info: Can be made into bread.

Potato

Food Points: Raw: 1, Cooked: 5

Seed: The potato itself.

Other info: Potatoes can also drop rarley from killing zombies.

Carrot

Food Points: 3

Seed: The carrot itself.

Other info: Carrots can also drop rarely from killing zombies.

Beetroot

Food Points: 1

Seed: Beetroot seed.

Other info: Beetroot can also be made into soup.

Melon

Food Points: 2

Seed: Melon seeds [made from melons].

Other info: You can make glistering melon by adding 8 gold nuggets.

Pumpkin

Food Points: 8 [when made into pumkin pie]

Seed: Pumpki seeds [made from pumpkins].

Other info: Pumpkins can also be made into pumpkin pie
by adding 1 sugar, and 1 egg.
