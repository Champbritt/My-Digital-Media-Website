More about Minecraft

Creative Mode

In creative mode you are given unlimited number of all minecraft blocks.

Here you will be able to build whatever you want without limitation.

Survival

In survival mode you will be able to put your survival skills to the test.

Starting out with nothing you will go throughout the world gathering resorces, building your own house, and battling monsters.
