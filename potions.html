This is the Potions Page












Awkward Potion:
Effect: Used to make other potions.
Recipe: Nether wartt + Water bottle = Awkward potion






Mundane Potion:
Effect: Used to make other potions.
Recipe: Spider Eye + Water bottle = Mundane Potion
Magma Cream + Water bottle = Mundane Potion
Redstone + Water bottle = Mundane Potion
Sugar + Water bottle = Mundane Potion
Rabbit Foot + Water bottle = Mundane Potion
Blaze Powder + Water bottle = Mundane Potion
Glistering Melon + Water bottle = Mundane Potion
Ghast Tear + Water bottle = Mundane Potion











Thick Potion:
Effect: Used to make other potions.
Recipe: Glowstone Dust + Water bottle = Thick Potion










Potion of Healing:
Effect: Restores 4 helth.
Recipe: Glistering Melon + Awkward Potion = Potion of Healing











Potion of Fire Resistance:
Effect: Gives protection from fire.
Recipe: Magma Cream + Awkward Potion = Potion of Fire Resistance










Potion of Regeneration:
Effect: Helps regain 2 helth every 2.5 seconds.
Recipe: Ghast Tear + Awkward Potion = Potion of Regeneration











Potion of Stength:
Effect: Increases player's attack damage.
Recipe: Blaze Powder + Awkward Potion = Potion of Strength










Potion of Swiftness:
Effect: Increases player's speed, sprint.
Recipe: Sugar + Awkward Potion = Potion of Swiftness











Potion of Night Vision:
Effect: Allows player to see at max light level.
Recipe: Golden Carrot + Awkward Potion = Potion of Night Vision










Potion of Invisibility:
Effect: Allows the player to become invisible, but all equipped item remain visible.
Recipe: Fermented Spider Eye + Night Vision = Potion of Invisibility











Potion of Water Breathing:
Effect: Allows the player to breath underwater without depleting your oxyhen bar.
Recipe: Pufferfish + Awkward Potion =Potion of Water Breathing










Potion of Leaping:
Effect: Increases the player's jump by about 1/2 block higher.
Recipe: Rabbit Foot + Awkward Potion = Potion of leaping











Potion of Slowness:
Effect: Decreases the player's speed, sprint.
Recipe: Fermented Spider Eye + Potion of Swiftness + Potion of Leaping = Potion of Slowness










Potion of Poison:
Effect: loses 1 helth every 2.5 seconds.
Recipe: Spider Eye + Awkward Potion = Potion of Poison











Potion of Weakness:
Effect: Decreases the player's attack damage.
Recipe: Fermented Spider Eye + Water Bottle = Potion of Weakness










Potion of Harming:
Effect: Instantly lose 6 helth.
Recipe: Fermented Spider Eye + Potion of healing + Potion of Poison = Potion of Harming
