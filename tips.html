This is the Survival Tips Page

Tip #1

When you first enter the game the first thing to gather is wood found from trees. Gather as much as you can before nightfall (Thats when the monsters come out).

Tip #2

On the first day before nightfall make a small house (7 X 7 X 3 works best) with a door. wait untill the sunrieses to go back outside again.

Tip #3

Stone can be found 4 blocks under dirt.

Tip #4

When stone is mined it turns into cobble stone, used to make stone tools, and furnaces.

Tip #5

You can sheer a Sheep for extra wool without killing the sheep (plus they look really funny when sheerd).

Tip #6

To venture to the nether it requiers a nether portal, Same goes for the End.

Tip #7

A neather portal is made from obsidian (lava + water).

Tip #8

Baby animals drop nothing when killed.

Tip #9

For an almost endless food supply fence in animals by what they are attracted to, breed them, and when theres to many kill a few for their food.

Tip #10

If you make a bed you will be able to skip night time.
